Chicken in Paris

Chicken

Description

A very easy recipe to make chicken in paris

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic, and onion.
  3. Place the chicken in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
